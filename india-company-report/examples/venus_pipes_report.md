# Venus Pipes

## Near Term (Next 1 to 2 Quarters)

- **Spooling Business Launch and Revenue Potential**: Management expects the spooling business to come on stream by December 2026, with the facility operational before that date. The revenue potential from this business is linked to the INR185 crore LOI from a single client, with further orders possible from the same or new customers. The capex of around INR70 crore is backed by this LOI, indicating high visibility and confidence in growth. "The order is a new one for us, but further, we are working on it and see more things to come."

- **Order Book and Visibility**: The current order book stands at INR450 crore, with additional LOIs and tenders in progress, providing good visibility for the next 2-3 quarters. "Apart from this, we had already LOI in some of the tender, which is volume of INR50 crores... we are expecting more orders from the BHEL side in the next 2 to 3 months."

- **Growth Guidance**: Management is confident of achieving more than 20% revenue growth in FY27, driven by new capacities, product expansion, and diversified markets. "We see it should be at least more than 20% growth... margin will be higher than last year, moving towards 17% and then 18% by FY28."

## Medium Term (6 to 12 Months)

- **Capacity Expansion and Product Diversification**: The company has completed its capex program, increasing capacities for welded and seamless pipes, and adding fittings. The expansion to 6,000 MT per annum for seamless pipes and tubes, along with backward integration, is expected to support long-term growth and improve operational efficiency. "The expansion significantly increased our product offering and strengthens our ability to serve our customers."

- **New Industry Opportunities**: Management sees emerging demand from sectors such as data centers, semiconductor manufacturing, solar, CNG/PNG, and oil & gas. The company has secured an LOI from the data center segment, reflecting confidence in capabilities. "We are receiving encouraging enquiries from these sectors and have already secured an LOI from the data center segment."

- **Order Flow from BHEL and Tenders**: The company expects to receive INR50 crore orders from BHEL within 30-45 days and anticipates more tenders opening in the next 2-3 months, supporting sustained growth. "We are already L1 in some of the tenders, which we expect to get in the next 1 to 1.5 months."

## Long Term (1+ Years)

- **Entry into Data Center and High-Value Segments**: The strategic move into the data center segment with a dedicated spooling facility aims to establish a leadership position in this high-growth industry. The business is expected to generate higher value addition, better realization, and margin potential. "Spooling solutions carry higher value addition, better realization and stronger margin potential."

- **Market Expansion and New Approvals**: The company plans to expand into new geographies, including the U.S., and expects to receive further approvals from oil & gas, chemical, and engineering sectors, which will support long-term growth. "We are open for data center opportunities worldwide, and we expect some approvals to convert in the coming quarters."

- **Sustained Growth in Export and Domestic Markets**: Despite current challenges, management remains optimistic about export growth, especially from Europe and the US, and domestic demand in power, oil & gas, engineering, and chemicals sectors. "We believe going forward also more than 30%-35% range should continue on the side of export."
